Namespace: CWGyroscope

CWGyroscope

CWGyroscope encapsulates gyroscope and accelerometer data for use in Connichiwa. It automatically receives the necessary data with the help of gyro.js and forwards the data by sending event:gyroscopeUpdate and event:accelerometerUpdate events.
